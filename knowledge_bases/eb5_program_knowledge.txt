The EB-5 Immigrant Investor Program, created in 1990, provides a path to permanent residency for foreign investors who make a substantial investment in a U.S. business that creates jobs for American workers.

Key requirements:
1. Minimum investment: $800,000 in a Targeted Employment Area (TEA), or $1,050,000 in a non-TEA area.
2. Job creation: Each investment must create or preserve at least 10 full-time jobs for qualifying U.S. workers.
